\section{What's The Trick?}
Consider a nonhomogeneous system of ODEs
	\begin{align*}
	Y' = AY + G .
	\end{align*}

The trick is to use a method called \textbf{variation of parameter}. 

Let $M$ be the fundamental matrix of $Y' = AY$. We suppose we have the matrix $M$ at hand.

\underline{Goal}: Determine a vector function $V$ such that $Y_P = MV$ is a particular solution to $Y' = AY + G$.
